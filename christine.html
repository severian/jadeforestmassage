About Christine

Christine Schoper, R.N., L.M.P., has been studying and practicing bodywork for 17 years. She is a graduate for the Scherer Institute of Natural Healing in Santa Fe, New Mexico. She has also worked as a registered nurse for 27 years with a B.S.N. from St. Olaf College in Northfield, Minnesota. Her practice focuses on promoting health and wellbeing through nurturing therapeutic massage with optional concentration on specific injuries and deep tissue bodywork.
